Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • (7 oz) beef steak or strips, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate Beef:Mix beef with soy sauce & pepper.
  2. Cook Beef:Stir-fry beef in hot oil, set aside.
  3. Veggies:Stir-fry onion, garlic & mixed vegetables.
  4. Add riceAdd rice, break clumps, stir well.
  5. Combine:Return beef & eggs, add sauces, toss.
  6. Finish:Add green onions & season to taste. Serve hot!
